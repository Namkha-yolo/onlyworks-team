§ 01 Start here

Day one. Build something tiny.

Your first week is a self-contained build exercise — a small todo app in our exact stack. It exists to prove you can ship in our system before you touch real OnlyWorks code. Don't try to make it impressive; make it work, and understand every choice.

§ 06 When you're stuck

The escalation ladder.

We don't punish disagreement; we don't punish breakage; we don't punish not knowing. We do notice silence — finding out in Week 6 that you've been blocked since Week 3 is the failure mode we're trying to avoid. Use this ladder in order.

  1. Read the error message. Sounds obvious; most people don't.
  2. Search the relevant doc. Cmd-F first. Most gotchas are in the guides.
  3. Search the official upstream docs. Tailwind issue? Tailwind docs. Not Stack Overflow.
  4. Search GitHub Issues of the failing package. Filter to the last 6 months.
  5. Ask in #engineering. 30-min rule. Include what you tried.
  6. Ping Namkha. Only after 1–5.
